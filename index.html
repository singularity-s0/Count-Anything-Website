Count Anything

A zero-shot object counter app. Currently only a demo of the technology.


Architecture
A ViT encoder followed by a convolutional decoder. The resulting number is the sum over decoder output. Image resolution is 384 x 384.


Note
This app only provide a rough estimation of the object count. Estimations may be inaccurate or incorrect. Do not rely on the results.
